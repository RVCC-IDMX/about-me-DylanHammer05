Biography

Hello there! I'm Dylan Hammermueller, a passionate individual hailing from Hampton, NJ. As a budding web developer, I find joy in translating ideas into digital realities.

My favorite way to work is during the quiet hours of the night, accompanied by the soft hum of creativity. I aspire to be a source of joy for others, whether through my code or the experiences I create.

In my free time, you'll find me immersed in various activities. I enjoy digital drawing on Procreate, crafting intricate pieces that often reflect my love for fantasy and storytelling. Playing the piano is another of my favorite pastimes, providing a melodic escape from the everyday hustle.

When the sun sets, I embark on virtual adventures with friends, engaging in intense gaming sessions that often lead to unforgettable moments. I also have a knack for skiing down tall mountains, seeking the thrill of conquering challenging slopes.

Oh, and don't be surprised if you catch me engrossed in a captivating horror novel or whipping up a storm in the kitchen experimenting with baking recipes. Life's too short not to explore every avenue of creativity!

If you're in need of a pick-me-up, check out this cute video that never fails to bring a smile to my face.
